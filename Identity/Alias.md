# Fergus Habberhand
Age: 54
Height: 6'2
Home Town: Carnrice
Profession: Toolsmith

Fergus Habberhand is a local repair shop owner in the town of Carnrice (carnris) in the the Siolbhanah wetlands. He has been the owner of Habberhand Hammerworks for the better part of the last twenty years. Fergus is friendly with the local community, but despite his healthy business with the townsfolk and near constant presence in town projects, there's no one in the village who can say they know too much about him.
