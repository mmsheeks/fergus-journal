## Caryl Craddoc
Medic | Elderly human woman
> Uses herbs and animal products found in the swamp to make salves & tinctures. 
> She's kind, but always seems a bit lost in her thoughts.

## Edwyn Craddoc
Fisher | Elderly human man
> His love for fishing is trumped by none, he doesn't often leave his chair on the dock. But if you want a good talk, joining him at the dock is always a fun time.

## Gwen Carnrice
Brewer | Adult goblin woman
> The Carnrices are well known for their signature brew; Fignen Brew, a slightly alcoholic tea made from local ingredients. This is her main job, but she also makes sure the tavern always has beverages for guests, either home made, or imported.

## Gwion Carnrice
Upkeep | Elderly goblin man
> In charge of keeping the buildings in working order, also oversees guests when they stay in the inn.

## Rhian Carnrice
Mayor | Elderly goblin woman
> In charge of dealing with people wanting to use Carnrice as a location for their event.

## Rhoslyn Carnrice
Cook | Adult goblin woman
> Makes the daily meals for the locals, and often also is hired to cook for the events.
